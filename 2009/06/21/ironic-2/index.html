Ironic

Posted on 2009-06-21 | Edited on 2019-02-27 | In Music

by Asteria C

听到一首很酷的歌，网上没有翻译的歌词，我试着翻译了一下，变成这个样子。
希望这个翻译的歌词可以给我带来多一些点击率。
然后我又想起那些，我自己寻找某些资料而偶然进去的博客，从来没有一个，因为那资料以外的东西而吸引住我。
而那些资料以外的，或许是他们真正想展示的东西。
汝之敞屣，吾之珍宝。
Isn’t it ironic?

Ironic

An old man turned ninety-eight 一个老头子，活到九十八
He won the lottery and died the next day 赢了乐透彩，第二天就挂
It’s a black fly in your Chardonnay 一杯夏顿埃，漂着只死虫
It’s a death row pardon two minutes too late 一道赦死令，晚到两分钟
Isn’t it ironic … don’t you think 是不是很讽刺，你觉得呢？

Chorus
It’s like rain on your wedding day 像你的婚礼，突然下大雨
It’s a free ride when you’ve already paid 像你付车钱，在免费之旅
It’s the good advice that you just didn’t take 像个好主意，你未曾听取
Who would’ve thought … it figures 谁都会觉得，那是天设计

Mr. Play It Safe was afraid to fly 怕死老先生，终生不飞行
He packed his suitcase and kissed his kids good-bye 收拾好行李，吻别众儿女
He waited his whole damn life to take that flight 活了这辈子，终于豁出去
And as the plane crashed down he thought 飞机坠毁时，他不免心语
‘Well isn’t this nice…’ 这可真够劲
And isn’t it ironic … don’t you think是不是很可笑，你觉得呢？

Repeat Chorus

Well life has a funny way of sneaking up on you 生活总突然，吓你一大跳
When you think everything’s okay and everything’s going right 当你过得好，一切没烦恼
And life has a funny way of helping you out when 生活总莫名，帮你个大忙
You think everything’s gone wrong and everything blows up 当你老碰壁，处处都搞砸
In your face

It’s a traffic jam when you’re already late 像你已迟到，又碰上堵车
It’s a no-smoking sign on your cigarette break 吸烟放松时，困在禁烟区
It’s like ten thousand spoons when all you need is a knife 你要小刀时，汤匙多的是
It’s meeting the man of my dreams
And then meeting his beautiful wife 遇到意中人，已有娇妻伺
And isn’t it ironic… don’t you think是不是很讽刺，你觉得呢？
A little too ironic… and yeah I really do think… 有些太讽刺了，我真的觉得

Repeat Chorus

Life has a funny way of sneaking up on you
Life has a funny, funny way of helping you out
Helping you out
它笑你也好，它帮你也好，人生难预料，处处有热闹
